Our Services

Providing strategic financial advisory and regulatory compliance services customized for startups, corporations, and NRI clients.

Taxation & Compliance

  • Direct Taxation: Annual Income Tax filing, tax planning, and optimization for businesses & individuals.
  • Indirect Taxation: Structuring tax liabilities, regulatory advisory, and state-level declarations.
  • Goods and Services Tax (GST): Monthly filings, annual reconciliation, GST audits, and refund representations.
  • NRI Taxation: Consultation on double taxation avoidance agreements (DTAA), asset repatriation, and filings.

Auditing & Assurance

  • Statutory Audits: Independent validation of financial state in compliance with the Companies Act.
  • Internal Audits: Evaluating corporate governance, risk assessment controls, and efficiency checking.
  • Tax Audits: Executing comprehensive audits under section 44AB of the Indian Income Tax Act.
  • Bank & Stock Audits: Conducting concurrent, branch, and stock verification audits for financial institutions.

Financial Consulting & Advisory

  • Business Advisory: Designing entry strategies, financial structuring, and business modeling.
  • Financial Advisory: Wealth advisory, project reports, debt-equity structuring, and funding assistance.
  • Financial Accounting: Outsourced bookkeeping, ledger drafting, MIS report creation, and balance sheet finalization.

Additional Services

  • Transfer Pricing: International transaction auditing, cross-border documentation, and local pricing studies.
  • Representation: Representing clients before Tax Authorities, Appellate Tribunals, and GST commissioners.
  • Incorporations: Fast-track Company, LLP, Trust, and Partnership registrations.
  • Secretarial Compliance: MCA filings, annual returns, board meeting document preparation, and CS compliance.
  • Transaction Advisory: Deal structuring, due diligence audits, and merger-acquisition accounting support.

Our partners offer personalized consultancies to draft tax-efficient strategies that scale seamlessly alongside your business operations.
