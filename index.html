About Us

N. A. Doshi & Co. is a Chartered Accountancy firm registered with the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI) and based in Mumbai. Established in 2002, the firm is led by a team of experienced and energetic professionals who bring both expertise and innovation to the field of auditing, taxation, and consultancy.

We are committed to providing high-quality, technology-enabled, and cost-effective professional services to clients of all sizes across various sectors. With a robust in-house training ecosystem, we nurture technical excellence and ethical practice among our team members.

We take great pride in the confidence and trust our clients place in us and are constantly striving to raise the bar for professional excellence.
