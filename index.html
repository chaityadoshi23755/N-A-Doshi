About N. A. Doshi & Co.

N. A. Doshi & Co. is a prominent Chartered Accountancy firm registered with the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI). Operating from Mumbai, the financial capital of India, our firm is led by a team of experienced and energetic professionals who bring both deep-seated technical expertise and fresh perspectives to the fields of auditing, taxation, and consulting.

Established in 2002, we have built a long-standing reputation for providing high-quality, technology-enabled, and cost-effective professional services to clients of all sizes across various industry sectors. With a robust in-house training ecosystem, we nurture technical excellence and ethical practice among all our team members to ensure unparalleled work standards.

We take great pride in the confidence and trust our clients place in us, and we are constantly striving to raise the bar for professional excellence in India's complex regulatory landscape.

Our Core Philosophy

Our Mission

To deliver reliable, premium professional services with utmost integrity, confidentiality, and technical excellence, tailored to meet every client's unique business needs.

Our Vision
